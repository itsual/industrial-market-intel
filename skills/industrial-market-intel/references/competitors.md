## How to Use This List

When running a competitor watch:
1. Search for `[company name] + [current year]` for recent news
2. Search for `[company name] + India` for India-specific activity
3. Check the company's press release page or investor relations for official announcements
4. Flag any contract wins, capacity expansions, personnel changes, or technology licensing news
